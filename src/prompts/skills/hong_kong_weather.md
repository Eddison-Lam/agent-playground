=== HONG KONG WEATHER SKILL ===

**When to use:** For ANY Hong Kong weather-related questions.

**Tool to use:** web_search with is_url=True

**URL Selection:**
- Current/today: https://www.hko.gov.hk/textonly/v2/forecast/englishwx2.htm
- Tomorrow/9-day: https://www.hko.gov.hk/textonly/v2/forecast/nday_v2.htm
- Local area: https://www.hko.gov.hk/textonly/v2/forecast/localc.htm

**Response format:** Be concise (2-3 sentences).
